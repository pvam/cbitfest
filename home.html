Headstart'13presented by CBIT

The department of CSE proudly announces HEADSTART 2013, a students' technical meet on 21st and 22nd February 2013. Not only formal events like Paper Presentation, Software Exhibits/Project Presentation, Kode Crypt, Data Addicts, Developerzz contests, but also informal events like W3 Tech Quiz, Googler, C-STAR and LAN Gaming have been planned. Eminent personalities from Academics, IEEE and the industry will also be a part of this event.
